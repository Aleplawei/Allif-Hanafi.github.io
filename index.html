I am caught in a paralyzing dichotomy between the asymptotic pursuit of perfection and the utilitarian necessity of completion. It is the classic struggle where the perfect becomes the enemy of the done; a state of analysis paralysis where the marginal utility of further effort has long since reached zero, yet I cannot let go.

I am Mohamad Allif Hanafi, a Graphic Designer haunted by the ghost of perfectionism—a relentless pursuit of the 'flawless' that threatens to dismantle the very career I have built.
